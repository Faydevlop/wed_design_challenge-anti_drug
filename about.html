About Us

Swathanthra is a movement that aims to make Kerala drug-free by educating and empowering the public to take action. We provide resources to identify, report, and prevent drug abuse, and offer support to individuals affected by addiction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a drug-free Kerala by raising awareness, offering resources for prevention and rehabilitation, and providing a platform for individuals to report suspicious activities. We believe that by working together, we can make a positive impact on our society.

What We Do

  • Raise awareness about the dangers of drug abuse.
  • Provide information about prevention programs.
  • Support individuals and families affected by addiction.
  • Offer resources to report drug-related activities.
  • Partner with local organizations and authorities to tackle the issue of drugs in Kerala.

How You Can Help

You can join us in this fight against drugs by spreading awareness, reporting suspicious activities, and supporting those in need. Together, we can make Kerala a drug-free state.
